# Prologue.
---
In a world of ruin, how does one exactly know what the truth is?
How does one know what is right and what is wrong?
Some were tired of these questions, they wanted an escape away from such which led to no answers, people who were so technically intelligent, but their soul would not comply to their harsh reality which led to answers of subjectivity.

In this world, subjectivity was undesirable to them, they wanted power, they wanted to feel like they could control, somewhere in their minds did they grow an idea of destruction.

The living of the world on the planet of EX Type 17 "Vertigo", or as they abbreviated it as EX-T17, questioned as they would, continuing to make breakthroughs in technology and science, these beings despite made of fragile flesh of simply being human, continually proved themselves and progressed into being so advanced that they could even participate in interplanetary research and transportation, but they had never found another living of their own anywhere until that point.


# Perplexity.
---
AD 2115, There were many competitions in the race of technological advancement and profit, until one day a corporation and also a faction of overall interest named Avez Technocrats (AvTC), found by a genius astrophysicist "Vanessa Avez" in AD 2100, detected abnormal activity from a relatively dormant unreported Artemis fragment as it had irradiated intense unpredictable thermal patterns caught by the AvTC's infrared sensor from the main observatory.
A research team of 2 talented physicists and 8 support agents were went to the site containing said fragment, a few months after this expedition, Avez gained access to extremely high power technology they originally named "Solstice A" and "Solstice B" due to energy extraction from the fragment.

It was one of the greatest breakthroughs in energy application, the Avez research division  further found the cause of variance in high thermal irradiation cycles but kept it highly confidential in order to manage monopoly by time they made it public, this helped them greatly in enhancing their weaponry power, but it introduced very shocking bottle-necks such as extremely high thermal throttling which made the weapons unusable, and very high recoil due to energy flux expended from the muzzle as well as the ignition from the chamber, one test firing even almost destroyed the metallic support pod it was mounted on during experimental shooting, therefore rendering it highly physically hazardous.

These problems slowly but surely made Vanessa desperate, however, her colleagues reported no sign of envy or competitiveness at the time, which was quite eccentric to them.

# A lifeless star.
---
AD 2130, Avez Technologies makes a deep dive into nearby Arctic ocean, upon traversing approximately 2750 meters and undisclosed directions underwater, they discovered an entirely different unknown glowing block of ice in solid state under a large iceberg, later research showed that moderate thermal exposure paired with very high pressure transitioned the solid into a super-fluid isotope of ice which perfectly assisted with the thermal issues
