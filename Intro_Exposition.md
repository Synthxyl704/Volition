
## Does an escape exist?
---
In a world of ruin, how does one exactly know what the truth is?
How does one know what is right and what is wrong?
Some were tired of these questions, they wanted an escape away from such which led to no answers, people who were so technically intelligent, but their soul would not comply to their harsh reality which led to answers of subjectivity.

In this world, subjectivity was undesirable to them, they wanted power, they wanted to feel like they could control, somewhere in their minds did they grow an idea of destruction.

The living of the world on the planet of EX Type 17 "Vertigo", or as they abbreviated it as EX-T17, questioned as they would, continuing to make breakthroughs in technology and science, these beings despite made of fragile flesh of simply being human, continually proved themselves and progressed into being so advanced that they could even participate in interplanetary research and transportation within their solar system, but they had never found another living of their own anywhere until that point.


## Perplexity.
---
AD 2115, There were many competitions in the race of technological advancement and profit, until one day a corporation and also a geographically polar faction of overall interest named Avez Technocrats (AvTC), found by a genius astrophysicist "Vanessa Avez" in AD 2100, detected abnormal activity from a relatively dormant unreported Artemis fragment in the Antarctic circle as it had irradiated intense unpredictable thermal patterns caught by the AvTC's infrared sensor from the main observatory.
A research team of 2 talented physicists and 8 support agents were went to the site containing said fragment, a few months after this expedition, Avez gained access to extremely high power elements they originally named "Solstice A" and "Solstice B" they had extracted from the fragment.

It was one of the greatest breakthroughs in energy application, the Avez research division  further found the cause of variance in high thermal irradiation cycles but kept it highly confidential in order to manage monopoly by time they made it public, this helped them greatly in enhancing their weaponry power, but it introduced very shocking bottle-necks such as extremely high overheating which made the weapons unusable, and also a very high recoil force due to energy flux expended from the muzzle as well as the ignition from the chamber, one test firing even almost destroyed the metallic support pod it was mounted on during experimental shooting, therefore rendering it highly physically hazardous.

These problems slowly but surely made Vanessa desperate, however, her colleagues reported no sign of envy or competitiveness at the time, which was quite eccentric to them.

## A lifeless star.
---
AD 2130, Avez Technologies makes a deep dive into nearby Antarctic ocean, upon traversing approximately 2750 meters and undisclosed directions underwater, they discovered an entirely different unknown glowing block of ice in solid state under a large iceberg, later research showed that moderate thermal exposure paired with very high pressure, then reheated at a specific high temperature and time, transitioned the solid into a super-fluid isotope of ice which perfectly assisted with the thermal issues, albeit with another issue which was undisclosed.

They named this super-fluid Isotope Axion (abbreviated as "ISAx" in official documents), it was named such due to later research confirming that the compound had been environmentally fabricated from the substances of an old asteroid strike which had deviated from an adjacent planetary system "Iris V", containing an exoplanet with an eccentric asteroid belt "Axion", further information of this was dissimulated from the public.

In order to resume smooth operations in the Antarctic bases without other factions intervening, Avez issued defense perimeters to various icebergs and landmass which legally made them  a property of Avez Technologies.

## avarice.
---
AD 2147, Avez Technologies had many internal disputes about open sourcing the research to other factions since years, this was mainly due to the astronomy division of AvTC wanting to expedite breakthroughs in energy propulsion and conservation technology for interstellar medium (ISM) travel, however this was never looked deep into as Avez was mostly a proprietary corporation. 

Avez developed systems which addressed the prior undisclosed bottle-neck of Isotope Axion usage in weaponry. Over the course of years, research had allowed them to analyze the properties of ISAx and Artemis extracted energy cores and they had a total monopoly over the information, production and selling of such technology to other factions.

Eventually, Vanessa's faction became the top-most economically leading faction, and she sought for more, her prior calm demeanour had finally transformed into something that wanted a competitive edge, as reported by her peers.

## Indifference. 
---
AD 2150, With an economical advantage, Avez invested their capital into infrastructure, issuing the creation of their first war weapon "Ether Axiom", and introduced many intra-factional doctrines. These doctrines were followed in an orthodox manner even for civilians, at some point they became so violently repulsive that Avez as a faction had lead in the loss of human capital due to riots by the people, and effective silencing of them by deploying armed units in order to "maintain faction peace".

Many homes were lost in the process of infrastructural development which had an intrinsic unofficial bias for R&D, Avez had lost its healthy ambition internally and attempted to conceal all of its issues from other factions via false reporting.

## Independence.
---
AD 2152, their false reporting would be uncovered eventually by espionage and reports from civilians of other factions, but Avez had already made significant progress in Ether Axiom along with more undisclosed issued weaponry research and advancements.

With such powerful autonomy and active transgressions in the Inter-factional Vertigo Union (IVU), Avez had departed from the Vertigo Union and became free from its monetary constraints, therefore expediting its technology research via autonomous expeditions and decision making in the matters of safety and security, this would backfire to some extent as some talented researchers had objections against the departure from the IVU and the security concerns from the expeditions.

## Eclipse war.
---
AD 2160, Avez continually invested into the Ether Axiom terrestrial mega-structure which spanned  approximately 450000 kilometers in length from the north coast of the Antarctic, heavy advancements made into Artemis fragment energy had given rise to mass production of multiple powerful weapons, along with a certain few highly destructive ones.

In late AD 2161, Avez had finally started actively attacking weaker factions first, then moving up slowly after complete annihilation of the prior ones.

Eventually the IVU was left with its last 7 factions out of the more than 15 it had originally, the remaining 7 factions resorted to using powerful banned and highly confidential Artemis cores which were later extensively engineered and open sourced into the IVU by a group of factions R&D centers to detonate into inhumane ultra-powerful EMP blasts for disarming and ionization irradiation for eradication of human life from most of Ether Axiom.

Despite inhumane amounts of retaliation from the IVU, Avez put forward an extremely valiant battle due to advances in property Artemis cores.

The war continued, with millions of people losing their spark of vitality every consecutive day.
Orphaned children, bereaved parents and the griefs of many despite such vehement experiences were not able to showcase even an ounce of mercy to the state of war.

There are many people who die everyday, people were so used to death that at some point it had become nothing to them.

The war however, continued.

Would it ever stop?
