### BNAP - 7 
For years, the race of advancement had given rise to corporations growing on a large scale.

Following the entrance of, and the fragmentation of the **Artemis** megastructure into Vertigo's planetary system **"Riftweave"**, corporations immediately seized the fallen **Artemis fragments** and issued perimeters around the impact sites under legal contracts which were bound with their government. 

It had become a common procedure for a period of time, a government would grow heavily dependent on a company due to internal corruption or a legal binding, pull them into a form of corporate supranational governance, and eventually the company's power would supersede the government, a new form of administration they coined as the **Overseer Administration (OA).**  

By AD 2020, many such countries were reformed by companies by winning the hearts of and making fruitful promises to their citizens, softly defaming the current governments which led to their power loss, and many different factions were formed instead with their own individual legal names.

However, factions would still compete with other factions in terms of military and power under the OA, for the greed of humans would not suffice to their content. Many factions surrendered upon losing to a stronger faction, and effectively had  themselves integrated into the winning faction.

### BNAP - 2
In AD 2025, the **Blitz Imperial State (BIS)** faction was a prominent influential entity within Vertigo, who were invaded and threatened super integration by the former **Kyoren Directorate (KyD)**  (now **Verdant Chroma Republic (VCR)**). In late AD 2025, a notable philosopher and an absolutist within the BIS would devise new names for a previously factional, romanticised futuristic naming system which re-framed the date notation and renamed terms for the Western / Georgian Calendar.

### NAP allotment 0 - The Union Foundation.
After the BIS found freedom from the VCR, the dating notations became more and more popular among the youth. The brave soldiers of BIS would collectively come together with multiple factions to form the **Interfactional Vertigo Union (IVU)** in AD 2027, consisting of over 27 factions and formally recognized the new notations as a mark of a new age, the **Neo Anthropic Present (NAP)**, although AD would not lose its relevance and would still be used interchangeably.

The foundation of the IVU reset the age to NAP "allotment" 0, factions under and part of the Union, that is, under the part of the **Overseer Administration** standard, would enjoy human life through issuing fundamental human rights, edicts and protocols in the endeavour of a utopian system.

### NAP allotment 15 - The Rise of  Avez Corporation.
Many factions flourished under the OA standard and made technological breakthroughs, with corporate respect to privacy and profit.

A faction however, of rather polar interest, would be founded in AD 2042, or NAP allotment 10, by a genius scientist **Vanessa Avez**. The **Avez Technocrats (AvTC)** situated their bases in the polar regions, on the northern pole, **Arctyrion** and on the southern pole, **Frosthelion**, with their main HQ being in the Frosthelion.

Along the poles would be the their respective circles stretching into kilometers of landmass of ice, glaciers, and some habitable sections. AvTC would research in the 2 polar circles under the OA standard, discovering many unreported and dormant Artemis fragments scattered around the polar landmasses

In NAP allotment 15 (AD 2047), Avez would discover an eccentric, abnormally large Artemis fragment site in a **northern part of the Arctyrion circle**, during a research expedition "Celerity Zero". The extraction and research of this Artemis fragment would change their fate.

The site was later notated and called as "Equinox", with 2 main extracted energy cores **Solstice A** and **Solstice B** which Avez would keep as proprietary, only allowing its transaction and usage for other factions after difficult extensive processing.

### NAP allotment 28 - Greed.
Avez made much progress with the Artemis deposits and especially with **Solstice A** and **Solstice B**. Being crowned the most technologically and economically advanced faction, it internally sought for more.

By NAP 28 (AD 2055), Avez had exacerbated its ties with the Union with infractions of the OA standards due to its wars with over 10 active non-IVU factions it handled alone, although it retaliated initially threatening to not meddle in the faction's independent factional issues, it was finally removed from the Union in late NAP 28 under the non-compliance to the OA standard.

### NAP allotment 30 - Avarice.
Avez did not forget their expulsion, and cut off all their transactions from the Union's factions as the Union issued currency would no longer be of use, and started increased its influx of independent research expeditions, with increasingly less hospitality towards its citizens and forced recruitments into military and research centers.

In NAP 30 (AD 2057), Avez issued the construction of the **Ether Axiom**, a large war pseudo-structure over the northern Arctyrion. Spanning over over 50 kilometers in length and 40 kilometers in width.

It would look like a great city, with divisions, complexes, and multiple centers solely for war operations, research, espionage and military. With its infrastructure arising over 5 kilometers in the sky above surface.

Acting as a barrier, the **Ether Cascade** spanned approximately 6.2 kilometers in length on the surface which wrapped the Ether Axiom's internal city, being its main military security. It had a strong EMP shield which rendered many other modes of reconnaissance useless, including orbiting satellites.

### NAP allotment 37 - Eclipse War.
Avez had made inhumane, rapid development of the Ether Axiom, which would be completed in within 6 years, and an extra year for functionality and testing.

In NAP 37 (AD 2064), Avez "killed" its first Union affiliated faction after its officials refused to integrate, what ensued was a declaration of war unto the Union under the name of **"Mission Total Eclipse"**, which aimed to completely eradicate the Union and establish its own separate order.

By NAP 40 (AD 2067), Due to Avez's constant barraged hellfire unto other factions, mass killing the lives inside and of over 10 non-Union factions and over 15 Union factions, with forcefully integrating some of them into itself. 

The last 12 factions on Vertigo, setting aside their differences, integrated closely into the IVU and made changes into its articles which governed the military, open sourced materials into itself, and fought collectively against Avez's terror.
